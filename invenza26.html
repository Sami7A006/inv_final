About the Event

WHERE IDEAS
TAKE FLIGHT

The AIML Club (2025–2026) of Geethanjali College of Engineering and Technology presents Invenza'26 — a flagship multi-day event that brings together the brightest minds for two high-energy competitions: Ideathon and Treasure Hunt 2026.

Spanning three electrifying days, Invenza'26 challenges participants to think boldly, collaborate fearlessly, and compete strategically — all in a structured yet thrilling environment designed to spark innovation and camaraderie.

The Competitions

TWO EVENTS.
ONE MISSION.

01
Innovation Track

IDEATHON
2026

A structured innovation challenge where teams pitch their boldest concepts or working prototypes. From problem definition to solution design, every idea gets its moment in the spotlight — followed by critical Q&A and expert feedback.

Concept & prototype pitching to panel judges
Live Q&A sessions with industry experts
Master classes on innovation frameworks
Structured time slots per team
Valedictory ceremony & award recognition
02
Adventure Track

TREASURE
HUNT 2026

A batch-wise, clue-based pursuit that demands teamwork, sharp logic, and raw speed. Navigate through a sequence of cryptic clues across campus, racing against rival batches to claim the final treasure first.

Batch-wise team competition format
Sequential clue-solving across campus
Tests logic, speed & team coordination
Progressive difficulty through stages
Race to the final hidden treasure
